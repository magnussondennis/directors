Directors list

Howard Hawks

Masterpiece: Only Angels Have Wings (1939), Rio Bravo (1959)
Highly recommended: Bringing Up Baby (1938), His Girl Friday (1940), To Have and Have Not (1944), Red River (1948), The Big Sleep (1946)
Recommended: Scarface (1932), Gentlemen Prefer Blondes (1953)

Akira Kurosawa

Masterpiece: Rashomon (1950), Ikiru (1952), Seven Samurai (1954), Throne of Blood (1957), Yojimbo (1961), High and Low (1963), Ran (1985)
Highly recommended: The Hidden Fortress (1958), Sanjuro (1962), The Lower Depths (1957), Rhapsody in August (1991), Dreams (1990)
Recommended: Sanshiro Sugata (1943), The Most Beautiful (1944), Sanshiro Sugata Part Two (1945), One Wonderful Sunday (1947), Scandal (1950), The Idiot (1951), I Live in Fear (1955), The Lower Depths (1957), Dodes’ka-den (1970), Dersu Uzala (1975), Madadayo (1993)
Approach with caution: The Men Who Tread on the Tiger’s Tail (1945), Song of the Horse (1970)

Orson Welles

Masterpiece: Citizen Kane (1941), Touch of Evil (1958)
Highly recommended: The Lady from Shanghai (1947), The Magnificent Ambersons (1942), Chimes at Midnight (1965), Mr. Arkadin (1955)
Recommended: The Trial (1962)
